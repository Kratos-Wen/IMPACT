Why IMPACT

Industrial procedural understanding needs task structure, not just clips.

Why existing datasets fall short

  • Weak procedural structure and limited recovery modeling.
  • Insufficient support for bimanual reasoning under partial observability.
  • Little separation between perception, reasoning, and forecasting failure modes.
  • Limited industrial realism for assembly-centric evaluation.

What IMPACT enables

  • Step-level and bimanual atomic annotations across a unified task space.
  • Cross-view temporal alignment and semantic matching under synchronized multi-view capture.
  • Short-term anticipation, long-horizon forecasting, and procedure reasoning under one benchmark.
  • Error analysis through compliance-phase and anomaly-type recognition.

Supported Tasks

Five benchmark groups align perception, reasoning, and forecasting.

Temporal Understanding

TAS-S · TAS-BL · TAS-BR

Dense temporal modeling with step-level and bimanual atomic labels.

Cross-view Understanding

CV-TA · CV-SMR · CV-SMC

Alignment and semantic retrieval across synchronized multi-view assembly videos.

Action Forecasting

AF-S · AF-L

Short-term anticipation is released now. Long-horizon forecasting is reserved and documented.

State & Reasoning

ASR · PSR

Assembly-state recognition and procedure-step reasoning on graph-constrained task structure.

Error Understanding

PPR-L · PPR-R · ATR-L · ATR-R

Phase and anomaly modeling for compliance reasoning and failure diagnosis.

Annotation Schema

Expose what is actually novel in the benchmark.

Temporal

Segment-level and frame-level annotations support `F1@{10,25,50}`, edit score, and dense evaluation.

Bimanual

Left-hand and right-hand atomic labels isolate asymmetric coordination effects.

State

Assembly-state annotations drive direct `ASR` and indirect `PSR` reasoning pipelines.

Reasoning

Procedure graphs, compliance phases, and anomaly types separate recognition from procedural inference.

Benchmark

Benchmark wrappers are organized by task and released progressively.

Released tasks

  • `TAS`: `LTContext`, `ASQuery`, `DiffAct`, `FACT`
  • `CV-TA` and `CV-SM`: `I3D`, `VideoMAE v2`, and `MViTv2` frozen encoders with cosine-kNN or linear probes
  • `ASR`: `MS-TCN++`, `VideoMAE v2+Head`, `Gemini 3.1 Pro`
  • `PSR`: `MS-TCN++ -> PSR`, `VideoMAE v2+Head -> PSR`, `STORM-PSR`, `Gemini 3.1 Pro`
  • `PPR`: `LTContext`, `ASQuery`, `DiffAct`, `FACT`
  • `ATR`: `LTContext`, `FACT`
  • `AF-S`: `AVT`, `ScalAnt`, `Qwen3VL-8B`

Metrics

  • `TAS`, `PPR`, `ATR`: `F1@{10,25,50}` and edit score.
  • `CV-TA`: retrieval and alignment scores.
  • `CV-SMR` and `CV-SMC`: retrieval and classification metrics.
  • `ASR` and `PSR`: task-specific recognition and reasoning metrics.
  • `AF-S` and `AF-L`: anticipation and forecasting evaluation metrics.
